Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish from Rome, known for its creamy texture and rich flavor. The dish traditionally includes ingredients like eggs, hard cheese (typically Pecorino Romano or Parmesan), pancetta or guanciale (Italian cured meat), and freshly ground black pepper. Unlike many creamy pasta dishes, Carbonara does not use cream; instead, the creaminess comes from the emulsified mixture of eggs and cheese, which coats the hot pasta. The result is a delicious, comforting meal that's quick and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti
  • 150g (5.3 oz) pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup freshly grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese (plus extra for serving)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente according to package instructions. Reserve about 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced pancetta or guanciale and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes. If using garlic, add it to the pan and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant. Remove the skillet from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated cheese until well combined. Season with a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked and drained, return it to the pot or a large mixing bowl. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the hot pasta, stirring vigorously to combine. The heat from the pasta will cook the eggs, creating a creamy sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add some reserved pasta water a little at a time until you reach the desired consistency.
  5. Stir in the crispy pancetta (and garlic if used) into the pasta. Mix well to ensure the pancetta is evenly distributed.
  6. Plate the Carbonara immediately, garnishing with additional grated cheese, freshly ground black pepper, and chopped parsley if desired. Serve hot.
